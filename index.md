## Biography
I am currently an honorary lecturer at School of Engineering, Australian National University, where I previously served as a postdoctoral fellow working with Prof Iman Shames. I received a PhD degree in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management (SEEM) in 2022, an MPhil degree in SEEM in 2018, and a BSc degree in Mathematics in 2016, all from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My PhD and MPhil degrees were both supervised by Prof Anthony Man-Cho So. My research focuses on optimization methods and its interplay with data science, signal processing, machine learning and operations research.
