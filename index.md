## Biography

I am currently an honorary lecturer at [School of Engineering](https://eng.anu.edu.au/), Australian National University, where I previously served as a postdoctoral fellow with [Prof Iman Shames](https://findanexpert.unimelb.edu.au/profile/537214-iman-shames). I received a PhD degree in [Systems Engineering and Engineering Management (SEEM)](https://www.se.cuhk.edu.hk/) in 2022, an MPhil degree in SEEM in 2018, and a BSc degree in [Mathematics](https://www.math.cuhk.edu.hk/) in 2016, all from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My PhD and MPhil degrees were both supervised by [Prof Anthony Man-Cho So](https://www1.se.cuhk.edu.hk/~manchoso/). My research focuses on optimization methods and its interplay with data science, signal processing, machine learning and operations research.
